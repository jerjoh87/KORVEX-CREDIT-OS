Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026 · Effective immediately

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the platform, we never sell your data, and you can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

CREDITOS ("we," "us," or "our") is a credit repair software platform operated as a SaaS service. Our platform is available at contentempireai.com and provides AI-powered dispute letter generation, credit report analysis, and client management tools for credit coaches and professionals.

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@contentempireai.com

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash). If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the platform, including features accessed, letters generated (not the content), and session timestamps.

Credit Report Data: When you upload or paste credit report text for scanning, this data is sent to our AI provider (currently OpenAI) for processing and is not permanently stored on our servers after the session ends.

Payment Information: Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full credit card number. We receive confirmation of successful payments and subscription status from Stripe.

Client Data: Information you enter about your clients (names, emails, credit scores, dispute history) is stored in your account and is your data. We do not access it except to provide the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the CREDITOS platform
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, payment receipts)
  • To improve the platform based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns
  • To respond to support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with anyone except as described in this policy.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • OpenAI — AI processing for letter generation and report scanning. Credit report text is sent to OpenAI's API and subject to their data handling policies.
  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Your account data is stored on Supabase's secure infrastructure.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. All payment data is handled by Stripe and subject to their privacy policy.
  • Lob — Physical mail fulfillment (optional). When you use the "Mail It For Me" feature, the letter content and mailing address are sent to Lob to print and mail the letter.
  • Netlify — Web hosting and CDN.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete all associated data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Credit report data processed through the AI scanner is not permanently stored — it is used only during the active session and discarded.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your client data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@contentempireai.com

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, and Supabase Row Level Security ensuring users can only access their own data. No security system is perfect, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

8. Children's Privacy

CREDITOS is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice in the platform. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests:
